DIU Admission

Admission to Daffodil International University is a straightforward process designed to welcome talented students. Applicants must complete the online registration form, submit required documents (transcripts, certificates), and pay the application fee. The admission committee reviews applications, and successful candidates are notified via email. Join us by applying today!

About DIU

Daffodil International University (DIU) is a leading private university in Bangladesh, established in 2002. Known for its innovative teaching methods, DIU offers a wide range of programs in technology, business, and humanities, fostering a dynamic learning environment with modern facilities and global partnerships.
